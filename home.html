How to make a great web application ?

What makes a successful web application? First and foremost, the application needs to fulfill the
intended business functions reliably, accurately, and productively. Next, maintenance of the
application needs to be simple and affordable. The user experience delivered must be positive.
And above all, the financial and functional benefits you receive need to outweigh the time and
money put into its development so your ROI is acceptable.
